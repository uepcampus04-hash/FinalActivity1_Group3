College of Accountancy

A College of Accountancy is a department or school in a university that focuses on teaching students how to manage, record, and analyze financial information, preparing them for careers in accounting, auditing, and finance.

College of Business Education

A College of Business Education is a part of a university that teaches students the skills and knowledge needed for business, such as management, marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship, preparing them for careers in the business world.

College of Criminal Justice Education

A College of Criminal Justice Education is a part of a university that prepares students for careers in law enforcement and public safety by teaching about laws, crime prevention, investigation, and the justice system.

College of Engineering

A College of Engineering is a part of a university that teaches students how to design, build, and improve structures, machines, and systems, preparing them for careers in fields like civil, mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering.

College of Hospitality Management

A College of Hospitality Management is a part of a university that trains students for careers in the service industry, focusing on skills in hotel management, tourism, food service, and customer care.

College of Information Technology

A College of Information Technology is a part of a university that teaches students how to use computers and technology to create, manage, and improve digital systems, preparing them for careers in programming, networking, cybersecurity, and IT support.

College of Midwifery

A College of Midwifery is a part of a university or school that trains students to care for pregnant women, assist in childbirth, and support mothers and newborn babies before and after delivery.

College of Teacher Education

A College of Teacher Education is a part of a university that prepares students to become teachers by training them in teaching methods, subject knowledge, and classroom management.
